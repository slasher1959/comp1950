About Me

Born to Ukrainian immigrants, I grew up in North Burnaby and from an early age was taughtthe importance of a strong work ethic and the value of investing in Real Estate.

I have been interested in and involved in real estate since obtaining my MBA at Simon Fraser University. Upon graduating, the first thing I did was purchase an investment property. From there, I grew my portfolio from Residential to include Commercial and Agricultural Real Estate that spans from Vancouver thru Richmond, Ladner, White Rock, South Surrey and Langley. While doing this, I obtained my license to sell residential, and then commercial real estate.

My team has had the number one standing in the Vancouver area in terms of volume and value of residential sales in 2011, 2013, and 2014.
